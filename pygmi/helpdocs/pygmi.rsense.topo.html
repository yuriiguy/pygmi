Topographic Correction

This module provides a correction for shadows caused by topography on imagery. The correction provided is the C-correction, as described by McDonald et al 2000. A digital elevation model must be provided with the satellite data. Solar azimuth and zenith information will be imported from the original satellite data format, otherwise it must be provided.

Indices

  • Digital Elevation Model - Band name of the DEM.
  • Solar Azimuth
  • Solar Zenith

References

McDonald, E.R., Wu, X., Caccetta, P.A., Campbell, N.A., 2000. Illumination correction of Landsat TM data in south east NSW. Paper Presented at: Proceedings of the Tenth Australasian Remote Sensing Conference.
